My name is Marcin Drozd and I am a self-taught developer. That's right, I woke up one day and decided that it's the high time to fulfill my long time dream.

I've been learning new things ever since. Actually, learning new things was the main reason I chose this path. Developers should never stop learning. On this blog, I share my struggles, things I've discovered or how simply what I read recently. I hope that it might be helpful, especially for those who as me are interested in this huge, sometimes scary world of programming.

I'm mostly writing Ruby on Rails apps, but I am also interested in JS (React) and Elixir.
